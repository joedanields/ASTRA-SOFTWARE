FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from prospective clients.

How quickly can you start on a new project?

We typically onboard new projects within 1–2 weeks of initial discussions. The timeline depends on project complexity and our current capacity. Contact us and we can give you an accurate start date.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Absolutely. While we are based in Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu, we work with clients globally. We are comfortable with async communication and can accommodate different time zones.

What does the project process look like?

We follow a structured 5-step process: Discovery → Design → Development → Testing → Launch & Support. You receive regular updates and demos throughout, ensuring complete transparency and alignment.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes. We offer flexible support packages ranging from bug-fix retainers to full managed service agreements. Our team is available for ongoing development, monitoring, and enhancement after launch.
